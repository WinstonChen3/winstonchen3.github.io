Q: What services do you offer at the makerspace?

A: We offer a wide range of equipment and services, including 3D printing, woodworking tools, electronics lab, and more. Check our Equipment page for a detailed list.

Q: Can I reserve equipment in advance?

A: Absolutely! Techlab allows members to reserve equipment in advance through our online platform. This ensures that you have access to the tools you need when you arrive at our makerspace.

Q: Is there training available for beginners?

A: Yes, Techlab provides training sessions for beginners. Our experienced staff conducts workshops and tutorials to help you become familiar with the equipment and unleash your creativity.

Q: What safety measures are in place at Techlab?

A: Safety is our priority. Techlab follows strict safety protocols, and our staff is trained to guide members on the proper use of equipment. Safety briefings and guidelines are provided to ensure a secure environment.

Q: Can I bring a guest to use Techlab equipment with me?

A: Currently, memberships are individual, and each member should use equipment individually. We may offer guest passes in the future.

Q: What happens if a reserved equipment is unavailable?

A: the rare event of unavailability, we'll notify you promptly and assist in rescheduling or finding suitable alternatives.
